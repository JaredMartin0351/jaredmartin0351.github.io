About Jared

Hey! So you found your way to my page, my name is Jared by the way and you are going to get to know a little about me here.

I started coding on my own when I was still in the military, I was a United States Marine for about 6 years. I went in directly out of high school. My family was all military, and it seemed like the right path for me. While I loved my time in service, my interests lead me elsewhere. I exited my time in service with an honorable discharge, multiple awards, and amazing leadership experience.

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science with an emphasis in Software Development and I am also attending a coding bootcamp with devCodeCamp and I AM LOVING IT.
